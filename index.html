üêÑ Goseva ‚Äì Sacred Cow Protection

At Sri Sri Radha Gokulanand Ashramam, Goseva is a sacred offering. We shelter native Indian breeds such as Gir and Sahiwal in our clean and peaceful Goshala, ensuring their comfort, health, and spiritual care.

Why Goseva?

Serving cows is a direct way to please Lord Krishna. It embodies compassion, sustainability, and spiritual merit. According to scriptures, Goseva bestows peace, prosperity, and liberation.

"Go Seva krutam punyam, bhukti-mukti-pradam nrnam."
"Serving the cow grants both material and spiritual prosperity."
